Placing Bets
  • Bets can range from a minimum of {{minimumAmountToBet}} {{token}} to any preferred maximum amount as long as the bettor has the funds in their wallet.
  • Players placing the bet on the game by entering the “Game ID or Game URL” must provide a valid “Game ID or Game URL” of an Active Game while placing bets. There have been multiple checks in place and the website ensures that players cannot bet on invalid / non-active game IDs, but if players try to spam the system, then they will lose all their funds associated with such activity.

    For a “Game ID or Game URL” to be considered valid, it must satisfy the following criterion: -
    1. The game with the provided “Game ID or Game URL” must exist on lichess.org .
    2. The game should not have ended by the time the bet is being placed on it.
    3. Game base time should be greater than 3 minutes .
      E.g.: Game with time duration 3 + 1 or 5 + 0 etc. (here 3 & 5 being base time) are considered valid , but game with time duration 1 + 0 or 2 + 1 or 2 + 5 etc. are considerd invalid .

Game End
  • Once the game has ended on lichess.org , its betting period will also end on chessBet.games , and within a few seconds, it will be available for claiming rewards, based on the results of the game on lichess.org.

Claim Reward
  • Bettors can manually claim their rewards/funds anytime by clicking on the “Claim Now” button on the website. If the bettor has more than or equal to 10 unclaimed games then rewards will be claimed automatically when they place their next bet.

Reward Calculation
Once the game has ended, the reward is calculated using the below formula.
  • Only the {{token}} that was bet on either side within the first 75% of the total duration of the gameplay time is considered for reward calculation.
    E.g.: If a game runs for 10 minutes, then only the {{token}} bet in the first 7.5 minutes will be considered for reward calculation.
  • Any other {{token}} will be returned (can be claimed back) BUT will also be exempted from any possible rewards. Votes associated with such {{token}} fall under the category of discarded votes.
  • Also if the final Playtime of the game is less than 60 seconds then all funds are returned back to the respective bettors.
  • All winning amounts that bettor claim will be reduced by {{feesAmount}} % as part of fees, which will be used to support the chessbet.games ecosystem, maintain smooth operation, and conduct events in the future.
